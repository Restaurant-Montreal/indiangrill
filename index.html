About us

Taste of flavors

Indian Grill (Varity of Currys,Dosas,Biryanis) is a worldwide popular due to their richness in ingredients and cheerful taste of each dish. Our Vision is to bring our famous & traditional home style healthy foods to the entire world by implementing uncompromised quality policy, real taste, use of natural ingredients and affordable price. Indian Grill has been accommodating the changes of time by increasing their standards to cater the ever-growing necessities of today’s taste hunters. We expanded into 2 different locations thanks to the continued support from the Montreal area customers.
